Vision

The Rotaract Club of NMIMS Sunrise envisions a society composed of individuals passionately serving and making a difference in the community.

Mission

The Rotaract Club of NMIMS Sunrise's passion for service and excellence is driven by Rotary International’s vision “service above self.” Thus, the Club commits itself to promote and support the advocacy of its sponsoring Rotary Club and District 3142, living by the Rotaract motto: fellowship through service.

Our Projects





1. Sahara - A dog welfare initiative

Sahara is a community service project for stray dogs. In Panvel where international airport is under-construction, dogs have been left abandoned, though humans have been shifted from there. There are few people who are helping those dogs but are expecting more help from clubs like us. The Rotaract club of NMIMS Sunrise helped them to collect funds. The club also took volunteers on the site for feeding and spending a day with them.



Name of the project: Sahara

Venue: Ulwe Kopar-Panvel (airport site)

Avenue: Community Service

Start time: 8th march 2019 (Friday) 3:30 pm - 6:30 pm

End date: 9th march 2019 (Saturday) 3:30 pm - 6:30 pm

Project level: Club Level

Frequency of the project: Once in a month



Aim/concept: To feed the stray dogs and cats who were left alone after the destruction of the site for the construction of the new airport.

Groundwork: Visiting the site twice for analyzing all the requirements for the volunteers and the animals.

Feedback/Follow up: A successful event for us and the volunteers. Though it was tough to the location. All the lovely dogs enjoyed playing with us.
