About Our Club




The Rotaract Club of NMIMS Sunrise is an institution-based club sponsored by the Rotary Club of Navi Mumbai Sunrise, Rotary International District 3142. It was chartered in December, 2018 under its first club president NISHAD KINHIKAR. It is currently a well- established youth organization committed to serving society towards a holistic development. RCNM sunrise values individuality, personal development and civic involvement as keys to improving society. It provides sustained projects as concrete opportunities for the formation of its members and volunteers. Setting high standards in all avenues of service, RCNM Sunrise upholds the practice of leadership through service and fosters an environment composed of competent and excellent Rotaractors.
